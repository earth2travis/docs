# The Marrow of Zen

_In the zazen posture, your mind and body have great power to accept things as they are, whether agreeable or disagreeable._

the worst horse is the most valuable one.

"to succeed wrong with wrong"

When you feel disagreeable it is better for you to sit.

In continuous practice, under a succession of agreeable and disagreeable situations, you will realize the marrow of Zen and acquire its true strength.
